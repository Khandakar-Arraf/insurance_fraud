Our Services

Insura's fraud detection system offers a variety of services to assist in identifying and preventing fraudulent actions in the insurance sector.

Data Analysis

Large amounts of data, such as policyholder information, claim records, financial transactions, and historical patterns, are analyzed by insurance fraud detection systems. They use advanced analytics approaches to spot abnormalities and odd patterns that could point to fraud.

Fraud Scoring

Based on their likelihood of involvement in fraudulent activities, these systems assign fraud scores or risk ratings to policyholders, claims, or other entities. The scoring algorithms take into account a variety of characteristics, such as previous claim history, data discrepancies, and strange conduct.

Claim Validation

Insurance fraud detection systems verify the legitimacy and correctness of policyholders' insurance claims. They compare claim data to historical data and other relevant databases to discover discrepancies or red flags.
